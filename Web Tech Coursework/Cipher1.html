Caeser Cipher

Here you can encode and decode messages written using Caeser's Cipher. This is a Cipher that simply swaps a letter with the letter 3 positions to the right of it in the alphabet.
This is one of the most famous cyphers ever used, widely known as Ceaser's own personal Cipher, He used this to hide the content of his messages from his enemies.

Please enter your message and how far you wish it to be shifted.



Key

Your message will be encoded after you press the encrypt button. To reverse it press the decrypt button.
